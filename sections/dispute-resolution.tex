\section{Dispute Resolution}
\label{sec:dispute}

The Parties agree to the following dispute resolution process for any disputes, claims,
or controversies arising out of or relating to this Agreement or the breach, termination,
enforcement, interpretation, or validity thereof.

\subsection{Good Faith Negotiation}
\label{subsec:good-faith-negotiation}

\subsubsection{Informal Resolution Requirement}
Before initiating formal dispute resolution proceedings, the Parties agree to attempt
in good faith to resolve any dispute through direct negotiation between the principals
of each Party.

\subsubsection{Negotiation Process}
Upon the occurrence of a dispute:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item The Party raising the dispute shall provide written notice to the other Party
          describing the nature of the dispute and proposing a resolution;

    \item Within seven (7) days of receiving such notice, the Parties shall meet (in person,
          by telephone, or by video conference) to discuss the dispute;

    \item The Parties shall negotiate in good faith for a period of thirty (30) days from
          the date of the initial notice;

    \item If the Parties reach a mutually acceptable resolution, they shall document the
          resolution in writing signed by both Parties.
\end{enumerate}

\subsubsection{Continuing Performance}
Except for disputes regarding non-payment or material breach, the Parties shall continue
to perform their respective obligations under this Agreement during the negotiation period.

\subsection{Mediation}
\label{subsec:mediation}

\subsubsection{Mediation Requirement}
If the Parties are unable to resolve the dispute through good faith negotiation within
the thirty (30) day period specified above, either Party may initiate mediation by
providing written notice to the other Party.

\subsubsection{Mediator Selection}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Within fourteen (14) days of the mediation notice, the Parties shall mutually
          agree upon a qualified neutral mediator;

    \item If the Parties cannot agree on a mediator within the fourteen (14) day period,
          either Party may request that a mediator be appointed by the American Arbitration
          Association (AAA) or a similar neutral dispute resolution organization;

    \item The mediator shall have experience in commercial contract disputes and, preferably,
          knowledge of the roofing or insurance industries.
\end{enumerate}

\subsubsection{Mediation Procedures}
\begin{itemize}
    \item The mediation shall be conducted in Oklahoma County, Oklahoma, or such other
          location as mutually agreed by the Parties;

    \item The mediation shall be governed by the Commercial Mediation Procedures of the
          American Arbitration Association or such other procedures as the Parties and
          mediator may agree;

    \item Each Party shall prepare and submit to the mediator a written statement describing
          the dispute and their proposed resolution;

    \item The Parties shall participate in the mediation in good faith and with authority
          to settle the dispute;

    \item All mediation proceedings shall be confidential.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Costs of Mediation}
The Parties shall share equally the fees and expenses of the mediator. Each Party shall
bear its own costs and attorneys' fees associated with the mediation, unless otherwise
agreed or awarded as provided in Section~\ref{subsec:attorneys-fees}.

\subsubsection{Mediation Outcome}
\begin{itemize}
    \item If the Parties reach a settlement through mediation, the settlement shall be
          documented in writing and signed by both Parties;

    \item Such settlement agreement shall be binding and enforceable;

    \item If the Parties do not reach a settlement within sixty (60) days of the mediator's
          appointment, either Party may proceed to litigation as provided in Section~\ref{subsec:litigation}.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Litigation}
\label{subsec:litigation}

\subsubsection{Jurisdiction and Venue}
If the dispute is not resolved through negotiation and mediation, either Party may
initiate legal proceedings. The Parties agree that:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Any legal action arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be brought
          exclusively in the state or federal courts located in Oklahoma County, Oklahoma;

    \item Each Party consents to the personal jurisdiction of such courts;

    \item Each Party waives any objection to venue in such courts;

    \item Each Party waives any claim that such courts are an inconvenient forum.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Governing Law}
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the
State of Oklahoma, without regard to its conflicts of law principles.

\subsubsection{Jury Trial Waiver}
\textbf{EACH PARTY HEREBY KNOWINGLY, VOLUNTARILY, AND INTENTIONALLY WAIVES ANY RIGHT
IT MAY HAVE TO A TRIAL BY JURY IN ANY LITIGATION BASED UPON OR ARISING OUT OF THIS
AGREEMENT OR ANY RELATED INSTRUMENT OR AGREEMENT OR ANY OF THE TRANSACTIONS CONTEMPLATED
BY THIS AGREEMENT.}

\subsection{Equitable Relief}
\label{subsec:equitable-relief}

\subsubsection{Right to Injunctive Relief}
Notwithstanding the dispute resolution procedures set forth above, either Party may
seek temporary restraining orders, preliminary injunctions, or other equitable relief
in any court of competent jurisdiction to:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Prevent irreparable harm;
    \item Enforce confidentiality obligations under Section~\ref{sec:confidentiality};
    \item Protect intellectual property rights;
    \item Prevent disclosure or misuse of trade secrets;
    \item Preserve the status quo pending resolution of the dispute.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{No Waiver of Other Remedies}
The right to seek equitable relief shall not constitute a waiver of the requirement to
engage in good faith negotiation and mediation for the underlying dispute, nor shall it
constitute a waiver of any other rights or remedies.

\subsection{Attorneys' Fees and Costs}
\label{subsec:attorneys-fees}

\subsubsection{Prevailing Party}
In any legal proceeding arising out of or relating to this Agreement, the prevailing
party shall be entitled to recover from the non-prevailing party:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Reasonable attorneys' fees and legal expenses;
    \item Court costs and filing fees;
    \item Expert witness fees;
    \item Costs of investigation and discovery;
    \item Any other costs and expenses reasonably incurred in connection with the proceeding.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Determination of Prevailing Party}
The prevailing party shall be determined by the court or arbitrator based on a comparison
of the relief sought and the relief obtained, and shall include a party who:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Obtains a judgment or award in its favor;
    \item Successfully defends against claims brought by the other Party;
    \item Obtains dismissal of claims brought against it;
    \item Prevails on significant issues in the litigation even if not the ultimate winner.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Continuation of Performance}
\label{subsec:continuation-performance}

Except for disputes regarding payment or material breach that would excuse performance,
both Parties agree to continue performing their obligations under this Agreement during
the pendency of any dispute resolution proceedings, unless:

\begin{itemize}
    \item The Agreement has been properly terminated in accordance with Section~\ref{sec:termination};
    \item A court or arbitrator has issued an order suspending performance; or
    \item The Parties mutually agree in writing to suspend performance.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Confidentiality of Dispute Resolution}
\label{subsec:dispute-confidentiality}

The Parties agree that all negotiations, mediations, and settlement discussions shall
be confidential and treated as compromise negotiations for purposes of applicable rules
of evidence. Neither Party shall disclose the existence or content of such proceedings
except:

\begin{itemize}
    \item As required by law or court order;
    \item To enforce a settlement agreement;
    \item To legal counsel, accountants, or other advisors bound by confidentiality obligations;
    \item With the prior written consent of both Parties.
\end{itemize}
