\section{Recitals}
\label{sec:recitals}

\whereas{the Company is engaged in the business of providing residential and commercial
roofing services, including insurance claim assistance for property damage}

\whereas{the Specialist possesses specialized knowledge, skills, and expertise in insurance
sales, insurance claim processing, adjuster negotiations, and materials procurement for
roofing projects involving insurance claims}

\whereas{for purposes of this Agreement, \defterm{Insurance Supplements} are additional
claims submitted to insurance carriers when the initial claim estimate misses items or damage,
which the Company will prepare and negotiate to secure additional payment}

\whereas{the Company desires to engage the Specialist to provide insurance sales training
and insurance claim processing services on an independent contractor basis}

\whereas{the Specialist will handle the overall insurance claim process including meeting
with adjusters and sourcing materials, while the Company will handle any insurance supplements
that arise from the initial claims}

\whereas{the Specialist desires to provide such services to the Company under the terms
and conditions set forth in this Agreement}

\whereas{the Parties desire to establish a mutually beneficial business relationship that
includes revenue sharing, knowledge exchange, and professional development opportunities}
