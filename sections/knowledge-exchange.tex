\section{Knowledge Exchange and Training}
\label{sec:knowledge-exchange}

The Parties recognize that mutual knowledge exchange and professional development are
essential components of their business relationship. Accordingly, the Parties agree to
the following training and knowledge-sharing obligations.

\subsection{Specialist's Training Obligations}
\label{subsec:specialist-training}

The Specialist agrees to provide training and knowledge transfer to the Company and its
designated employees in the following areas:

\subsubsection{Sales and Customer Relationship Management}
The Specialist shall train Company personnel in:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Effective sales techniques for roofing services and insurance claim assistance;
    \item Customer communication strategies and relationship building;
    \item Consultative selling approaches for residential and commercial clients;
    \item Handling customer objections and concerns;
    \item Closing techniques and follow-up procedures;
    \item Lead generation and qualification methods.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Insurance Supplement Expertise}
The Specialist shall share knowledge and best practices regarding:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Insurance supplement claim preparation and documentation;
    \item Effective negotiation strategies with insurance adjusters;
    \item Industry standards and acceptable practices in supplement claims;
    \item Common insurance carrier objections and how to address them;
    \item Maximizing claim value while maintaining ethical standards;
    \item Identifying supplement opportunities during property inspections.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Training Format and Schedule}
The Specialist's training obligations shall be fulfilled through:
\begin{itemize}
    \item On-the-job training during actual customer interactions and claim preparations;
    \item Periodic formal training sessions (at least monthly during Trial Period);
    \item Job shadowing opportunities where Company personnel observe the Specialist;
    \item Providing reference materials, templates, and resources;
    \item Answering questions and providing guidance as needed.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Company's Training Obligations}
\label{subsec:company-training}

The Company agrees to provide training and knowledge transfer to the Specialist in the
following areas:

\subsubsection{Clerical and Administrative Operations}
The Company shall train the Specialist in:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Office procedures and administrative workflows;
    \item Document management and filing systems;
    \item Customer database management and record-keeping;
    \item Scheduling and calendar management;
    \item Email and communication protocols;
    \item Office software and technology tools used by the Company.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Roofing Business Operations}
The Company shall share knowledge regarding:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Estimating procedures and pricing structures;
    \item Project management and job tracking;
    \item Vendor and supplier relationships;
    \item Quality control and job completion standards;
    \item Regulatory compliance and permitting requirements;
    \item Customer service policies and procedures.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Training Format and Schedule}
The Company's training obligations shall be fulfilled through:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Hands-on training at the Company's office location;
    \item Access to the Company's administrative systems and software;
    \item Providing written procedures and process documentation;
    \item Periodic training sessions (at least monthly during Trial Period);
    \item Ongoing support and assistance as questions arise.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Time Commitment}
\label{subsec:training-time}

\subsubsection{Training Hours}
Each Party agrees to dedicate reasonable time to fulfill their training obligations.
During the Trial Period, the Parties anticipate the following minimum time commitments:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Specialist Training Provided:} Minimum of 8 hours per month during Trial Period;
    \item \textbf{Company Training Provided:} Minimum of 8 hours per month during Trial Period.
\end{itemize}

After the Trial Period, training shall continue on an as-needed basis, with each Party
making themselves reasonably available to answer questions and provide guidance.

\subsubsection{Compensation for Training Time}
Training time is considered part of the overall business relationship established by
this Agreement. No separate compensation shall be paid for time spent providing or
receiving training, as the mutual knowledge exchange is reflected in the revenue
sharing structure defined in Section~\ref{sec:compensation}.

\subsection{Confidentiality of Training Materials}
\label{subsec:training-confidentiality}

All information, techniques, methods, and materials shared during training sessions
shall be considered Confidential Information as defined in Section~\ref{sec:confidentiality}
and shall be protected accordingly.

\subsection{No Guarantee of Results}
\label{subsec:training-disclaimer}

While both Parties agree to provide training in good faith and to the best of their
abilities, neither Party guarantees specific results or outcomes from the training
provided. Each Party acknowledges that the application and effectiveness of training
depends on numerous factors beyond the trainer's control.
