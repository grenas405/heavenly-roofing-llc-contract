\section{Knowledge Exchange and Training}
\label{sec:knowledge-exchange}

The Parties recognize that mutual knowledge exchange and professional development are
essential components of their business relationship. The Specialist's primary role is to
train the Company in sales, customer acquisition, and supplement negotiation, while the
Company provides training in clerical and administrative operations.

\subsection{Specialist's Training Obligations}
\label{subsec:specialist-training}

The Specialist's \textbf{primary role} under this Agreement is to provide comprehensive
training and knowledge transfer to the Company and its designated employees. The Specialist
agrees to train Company personnel in the following areas:

\subsubsection{Insurance Sales Training (Primary Focus)}
\label{subsubsec:insurance-sales-training}

The Specialist shall provide extensive training to Company personnel in insurance sales
techniques and strategies, including:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Insurance claim sales techniques and strategies for roofing services;
    \item Identifying properties with insurance claim opportunities (storm damage, hail, wind);
    \item Building relationships with homeowners who have insurance claims;
    \item Navigating the insurance claim sales process from lead to contract;
    \item Converting insurance claim leads into roofing contracts;
    \item Working with homeowners through the insurance claim journey;
    \item Understanding homeowner concerns about filing insurance claims;
    \item Explaining the insurance claim process to potential customers;
    \item Identifying storm-damaged properties and generating claim-based leads;
    \item Ethics and compliance in insurance claim-based sales;
    \item Building long-term customer relationships through claim assistance;
    \item Following up with insurance claim prospects effectively.
\end{itemize}

This insurance sales training is the cornerstone of the Specialist's obligations under
this Agreement.

\subsubsection{Insurance Claim Processing Expertise}
The Specialist shall also share knowledge and best practices regarding insurance claim
processing and adjuster negotiations, including:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Insurance claim filing and documentation procedures;
    \item Working effectively with insurance adjusters during inspections;
    \item Damage assessment and claim estimation techniques;
    \item Materials procurement processes for insurance jobs;
    \item Claim negotiation strategies and best practices;
    \item Common insurance adjuster objections and how to address them;
    \item Maximizing initial claim value while maintaining ethical standards;
    \item Proper documentation and photography of property damage.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Specialist's Ongoing Service Responsibilities}
In addition to training obligations, the Specialist shall continue to provide direct service
by processing insurance claims for the Company's customers, meeting with adjusters, sourcing
materials, and applying the expertise described above to maximize initial claim approvals.

\subsubsection{Training Format and Schedule}
The Specialist's training obligations shall be fulfilled through:
\begin{itemize}
    \item On-the-job training during actual customer interactions and claim preparations;
    \item Periodic formal training sessions (at least monthly during Trial Period);
    \item Job shadowing opportunities where Company personnel observe the Specialist;
    \item Providing reference materials, templates, and resources;
    \item Answering questions and providing guidance as needed.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Company's Training Obligations}
\label{subsec:company-training}

In exchange for the Specialist's sales and supplement training, the Company agrees to
provide training and knowledge transfer to the Specialist in clerical, administrative,
and operational areas:

\subsubsection{Clerical Side of Insurance Work in Roofing}
The Company shall train the Specialist in:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Insurance claim paperwork and documentation workflows;
    \item Filing systems for insurance claim files and records;
    \item Tracking claim status and follow-up procedures;
    \item Insurance carrier portals and online claim systems;
    \item Claim estimate software and tools specific to roofing;
    \item Administrative procedures specific to insurance roofing work;
    \item Regulatory compliance and documentation requirements for insurance claims;
    \item Record-keeping for insurance-related roofing projects.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Roofing Business Operations}
The Company shall share knowledge regarding:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Estimating procedures and pricing structures for insurance jobs;
    \item Project management and job tracking for insurance claims;
    \item Vendor and supplier relationships for materials procurement;
    \item Materials sourcing and ordering processes;
    \item Quality control and job completion standards;
    \item Regulatory compliance and permitting requirements;
    \item Customer service policies and procedures for insurance customers.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Training Format and Schedule}
The Company's training obligations shall be fulfilled through:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Hands-on training at the Company's office location;
    \item Access to the Company's administrative systems and software;
    \item Providing written procedures and process documentation;
    \item Periodic training sessions (at least monthly during Trial Period);
    \item Ongoing support and assistance as questions arise.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Time Commitment}
\label{subsec:training-time}

\subsubsection{Training Hours}
Each Party agrees to dedicate reasonable time to fulfill their training obligations,
recognizing that the Specialist's training role is a primary component of this Agreement.
During the Trial Period, the Parties anticipate the following minimum time commitments:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Specialist Training Provided:} Minimum of 8 hours per month during Trial
          Period, with primary focus on insurance sales training;
    \item \textbf{Company Training Provided:} Minimum of 8 hours per month during Trial Period.
\end{itemize}

After the Trial Period, training shall continue on an as-needed basis, with each Party
making themselves reasonably available to answer questions and provide guidance. The
Specialist's ongoing obligation to teach insurance sales techniques remains a core
component of the business relationship.

\subsubsection{Compensation for Training Time}
The Specialist's training responsibilities and the Company's training obligations are
integral parts of the overall business relationship established by this Agreement. No
separate compensation shall be paid for time spent providing or receiving training, as
the mutual knowledge exchange is reflected in the revenue sharing structure defined in
Section~\ref{sec:compensation}.

\subsection{Confidentiality of Training Materials}
\label{subsec:training-confidentiality}

All information, techniques, methods, and materials shared during training sessions
shall be considered Confidential Information as defined in Section~\ref{sec:confidentiality}
and shall be protected accordingly.

\subsection{No Guarantee of Results}
\label{subsec:training-disclaimer}

While both Parties agree to provide training in good faith and to the best of their
abilities, neither Party guarantees specific results or outcomes from the training
provided. Each Party acknowledges that the application and effectiveness of training
depends on numerous factors beyond the trainer's control.
