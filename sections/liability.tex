\section{Liability and Indemnification}
\label{sec:liability}

\subsection{Independent Contractor Status}
\label{subsec:independent-contractor}

\subsubsection{Relationship of Parties}
The Parties expressly agree and acknowledge that:

\begin{itemize}
    \item The Specialist is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, partner,
          or joint venturer of the Company;

    \item The Specialist has no authority to bind the Company or incur any obligation on
          behalf of the Company without the Company's prior written consent;

    \item The Specialist shall have sole control over the manner and means of performing
          services under this Agreement, subject to the specifications and requirements
          set forth herein;

    \item Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed to create an employment relationship,
          partnership, or joint venture between the Parties.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Tax and Regulatory Obligations}
The Specialist acknowledges and agrees that:

\begin{itemize}
    \item The Specialist is solely responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes,
          including self-employment taxes, arising from compensation received under this Agreement;

    \item The Company will not withhold income taxes, Social Security taxes, unemployment
          insurance contributions, or any other payroll taxes from payments to the Specialist;

    \item The Company will issue IRS Form 1099-NEC (or applicable form) to the Specialist
          for all payments made during each calendar year;

    \item The Specialist is responsible for obtaining and maintaining any licenses,
          permits, or certifications required to perform services under this Agreement;

    \item The Specialist is responsible for maintaining their own business insurance,
          including general liability and professional liability coverage as appropriate.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{No Employee Benefits}
The Specialist acknowledges that they are not entitled to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Health insurance or other employee benefits;
    \item Workers' compensation coverage;
    \item Unemployment insurance benefits;
    \item Paid time off, vacation, or sick leave;
    \item Participation in any Company retirement or pension plans;
    \item Any other benefits provided to Company employees.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Indemnification by Specialist}
\label{subsec:specialist-indemnification}

\subsubsection{Indemnification Obligation}
The Specialist agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Company, its officers,
directors, members, employees, and agents (collectively, the \defterm{Company Indemnitees})
from and against any and all claims, damages, losses, liabilities, costs, and expenses
(including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or resulting from:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item The Specialist's negligent acts or omissions in performing services under this Agreement;

    \item The Specialist's willful misconduct, fraud, or violation of law;

    \item The Specialist's breach of any representation, warranty, or obligation in this Agreement;

    \item Claims by third parties alleging that the Specialist's services or work product
          infringes any intellectual property rights;

    \item The Specialist's violation of any applicable law, regulation, or professional standard;

    \item Claims by tax authorities or governmental agencies arising from the Specialist's
          status as an independent contractor;

    \item Any bodily injury, property damage, or other harm caused by the Specialist's
          actions or omissions.
\end{enumerate}

\subsubsection{Defense of Claims}
The Specialist shall, at their own expense, defend any claim or action brought against
the Company Indemnitees with respect to matters subject to indemnification under this
Section, provided that the Company:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Provides prompt written notice of the claim to the Specialist;
    \item Cooperates reasonably with the Specialist in the defense;
    \item Permits the Specialist to control the defense and settlement of the claim,
          subject to the Company's approval of any settlement (approval not to be
          unreasonably withheld).
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Indemnification by Company}
\label{subsec:company-indemnification}

\subsubsection{Indemnification Obligation}
The Company agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Specialist and the
Specialist's employees and agents (collectively, the \defterm{Specialist Indemnitees})
from and against any and all claims, damages, losses, liabilities, costs, and expenses
(including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or resulting from:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item The Company's negligent acts or omissions unrelated to services provided by the Specialist;

    \item The Company's willful misconduct, fraud, or violation of law;

    \item The Company's breach of any representation, warranty, or obligation in this Agreement;

    \item Claims by customers or third parties arising from the Company's roofing work
          (as opposed to the Specialist's supplement services);

    \item The Company's failure to pay suppliers, subcontractors, or employees;

    \item The Company's violation of any applicable law or regulation in the conduct of
          its roofing business.
\end{enumerate}

\subsubsection{Defense of Claims}
The Company shall, at its own expense, defend any claim or action brought against the
Specialist Indemnitees with respect to matters subject to indemnification under this
Section, provided that the Specialist:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Provides prompt written notice of the claim to the Company;
    \item Cooperates reasonably with the Company in the defense;
    \item Permits the Company to control the defense and settlement of the claim, subject
          to the Specialist's approval of any settlement (approval not to be unreasonably withheld).
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Limitation of Liability}
\label{subsec:limitation-liability}

\subsubsection{Cap on Damages}
Except for claims arising from:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Breach of confidentiality obligations (Section~\ref{sec:confidentiality});
    \item Fraud, willful misconduct, or gross negligence;
    \item Indemnification obligations (Sections \ref{subsec:specialist-indemnification}
          and \ref{subsec:company-indemnification});
    \item Unpaid compensation owed under Section~\ref{sec:compensation};
\end{itemize}

\noindent Each Party's total liability to the other Party under this Agreement shall not exceed
the total amount of compensation paid or payable to the Specialist during the twelve (12)
months preceding the claim.

\subsubsection{Exclusion of Consequential Damages}
Except for breaches of confidentiality obligations, neither Party shall be liable to the
other Party for any indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or punitive damages,
including but not limited to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Lost profits or revenue;
    \item Loss of business opportunities;
    \item Loss of goodwill or reputation;
    \item Cost of substitute services or products;
    \item Loss of data or information;
\end{itemize}

\noindent even if such Party has been advised of the possibility of such damages.

\subsubsection{Direct Damages Only}
Subject to the exclusions above, each Party's liability shall be limited to direct
damages actually incurred and proven with reasonable certainty.

\subsection{Insurance}
\label{subsec:insurance}

\subsubsection{Specialist's Insurance}
The Specialist is encouraged, but not required, to maintain appropriate insurance coverage,
including:
\begin{itemize}
    \item General liability insurance;
    \item Professional liability (errors and omissions) insurance;
    \item Commercial automobile insurance if using a vehicle for business purposes.
\end{itemize}

The Company may request certificates of insurance, and the Specialist agrees to provide
them if such insurance is maintained.

\subsubsection{Company's Insurance}
The Company represents that it maintains appropriate insurance coverage for its roofing
business operations, including general liability and workers' compensation insurance.

\subsubsection{No Additional Insured Requirement}
Neither Party is required to name the other Party as an additional insured on any insurance
policy unless specifically agreed in writing.

\subsection{Limitation on Third-Party Beneficiaries}
\label{subsec:no-third-party-beneficiaries}

This Agreement is solely for the benefit of the Parties and their respective successors
and permitted assigns. Except as expressly provided in the indemnification provisions
above, no third party shall have any rights or remedies under this Agreement.
