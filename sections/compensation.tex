\section{Compensation Structure}
\label{sec:compensation}

\subsection{Standard Revenue Split}
\label{subsec:standard-split}

For each insurance supplement claim successfully collected by the Company, the revenue
shall be divided between the Parties as follows:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Specialist Compensation:} The Specialist shall receive \percentage{70}
          (seventy percent) of the gross amount collected from the insurance supplement claim.

    \item \textbf{Company Compensation:} The Company shall receive \percentage{30}
          (thirty percent) of the gross amount collected from the insurance supplement claim.
\end{enumerate}

This Standard Revenue Split applies to all supplement claims below the Big Supplement
Threshold defined in Section~\ref{subsec:big-supplement}.

\subsection{Big Supplement Split}
\label{subsec:big-supplement}

\subsubsection{Definition of Big Supplement}
A \defterm{Big Supplement} is any individual insurance supplement claim where the gross
amount collected from the insurance carrier equals or exceeds \moneythreshold{10,000.00}
(Ten Thousand Dollars).

\subsubsection{Big Supplement Revenue Split}
For each Big Supplement successfully collected by the Company, the revenue shall be
divided equally between the Parties as follows:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Specialist Compensation:} The Specialist shall receive \percentage{50}
          (fifty percent) of the gross amount collected from the Big Supplement.

    \item \textbf{Company Compensation:} The Company shall receive \percentage{50}
          (fifty percent) of the gross amount collected from the Big Supplement.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Payment Terms}
\label{subsec:payment-terms}

\subsubsection{Payment Timing}
The Company shall pay the Specialist their share of any supplement claim within
\textbf{fifteen (15) business days} following the Company's receipt of payment from
the insurance carrier.

\subsubsection{Payment Method}
Unless otherwise agreed in writing, all payments shall be made by:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Check mailed to the Specialist's address listed in Section~\ref{sec:parties}; or
    \item Electronic transfer (ACH or wire transfer) to the Specialist's designated bank account; or
    \item Such other method as mutually agreed upon in writing by the Parties.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Payment Documentation}
With each payment, the Company shall provide the Specialist with documentation including:
\begin{itemize}
    \item The customer name and property address;
    \item The insurance carrier and claim number;
    \item The gross amount collected from the insurance carrier;
    \item The calculation of the Specialist's share;
    \item The date payment was received from the insurance carrier.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Gross Amount Definition}
\label{subsec:gross-amount}

For purposes of this Agreement, \defterm{Gross Amount Collected} means the total amount
paid by the insurance carrier for the supplement claim, before deduction of any fees,
costs, or expenses, but excluding:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Sales tax or other taxes;
    \item Amounts designated by the insurance carrier for general overhead and profit
          that were part of the original claim (not the supplement);
    \item Any amounts collected that are later refunded or charged back by the insurance carrier.
\end{itemize}
