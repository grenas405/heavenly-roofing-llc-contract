\section{Term and Scope of Services}
\label{sec:term}

\subsection{Engagement}
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue until
terminated by either party in accordance with Section~\ref{sec:termination} of this Agreement.

\subsection{Services}
The Specialist shall provide the following services to the Company:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Primary Focus -- Sales and Customer Acquisition Training:} Train Company
          personnel in sales techniques, customer acquisition strategies, lead generation,
          and customer relationship management to help Heavenly Roofing grow its customer base;
    \item Prepare and negotiate insurance supplement claims to maximize recovery;
    \item Review and analyze insurance claims related to roofing damage;
    \item Obtain materials, estimates, and documentation necessary for insurance supplement claims;
    \item Meet with insurance adjusters and conduct on-site inspections as needed;
    \item Communicate with insurance adjusters and carriers on behalf of the Company;
    \item Document and photograph property damage to support supplement claims;
    \item Provide expertise and guidance on insurance claim procedures and best practices;
    \item Such other related services as may be mutually agreed upon by the parties.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Independent Contractor Relationship}
The parties expressly agree that the Specialist is an independent contractor and not
an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of the Company. The Specialist shall
have no authority to bind the Company or incur any obligation on behalf of the Company
without the Company's prior written consent.
